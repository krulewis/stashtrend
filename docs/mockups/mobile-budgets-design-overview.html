1 — Full View Mockups
Three horizontally-swipeable views rendered at 375px mobile viewport width. View 1 (Month Detail) is the default landing view. View 2 (Monthly Summary) is reached by swiping right. Dot indicators live in a fixed bar at the bottom that budget cards never scroll into.
